Human Resource & Office Manager

 

POSITION TITLE Human Resource & Office Manager
REPORTS TO General Manager (business reporting)
VP HR (functional reporting)
BUSINESS AREA/ COMPANY/ DEPARTMENT Human Resources
LOCATION Cebu, Philippines
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES No direct reports, but acts as a guide to employees and leadership

ESSENTIAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty and responsibility satisfactorily:

Leadership advisory role for Global Zeal management team providing guidance to leadership in basic employee relations issues, including employee coaching and counseling, documenting employee performance, and termination procedures
Manages the recruitment process for all Global Zeal, including job posting, candidate screening, interviewing, progress reporting, and orientation. Prepares hire and promotion documents
Administers compensation and benefit programs, ensuring compliance with local regulation and legislation while aligned with SINET culture
Assists leadership with the annual performance management review program, including leadership consultation and data management to ensure effectiveness, compliance and equity within the organization
Promote workplace diversity through fair hiring practices
Performs all HR filings (benefits, HR legal compliance, etc.)
Responsible for HR system maintenance and updates
Designs and implements employee and leadership training including policies, communication, performance management, benefits, etc.
Participates in special projects for HR as needed
Payroll responsibility to include data entry, ensuring pay is on-time, accurate and approved
Communicate with management and employees to answer questions, clarify policies and processes, create solutions and resolve issues or conflicts
Office management and support for the Cebu location including ordering office supplies, coordinating with building services, other items as determined by FM, etc.
Supports corporate initiatives and communicates to leadership and employees as appropriate
Health and safety coordination in compliance with local laws and regulations

REQUIRED EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE

A university degree from an accredited university, plus four to five years of directly related HR generalist/leadership experience in a similarly complex HR environment. Prior work experience to include working with US based companies. Labour Relations experience in a leadership capacity including grievance procedure. Bi-lingual (English and XXXX) preferred.

OTHER QUALIFICATIONS, SKILLS & ABILITIES

The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.

Highly developed sensibilities around (corporate) cultural nuances in order to effectively influence stakeholders and maximize organizational potential
Ability to work with US based leadership and local leadership in a coordinating and collaborative manner
Payroll experience is required
Proficient in MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook
Develop and run basic reports out of databases
Ability to maintain confidentiality and build strong relationships with a diverse workforce
Objectively coach employees and management through complex, difficult, and emotional issues
Make recommendations that effectively resolve problems by using judgment that is consistent with standards, practices, policies, procedures, regulation, or law
Exercises good judgment and effectively uses decision making skills. Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
Ability to travel including internationally if required

About

School Improvement Network (Global Zeal Inc. Cebu) is an online professional development and teacher training company that partners with schools, districts, and educators throughout the United States, Canada, and overseas to increase student achievement.

School Improvement Network believes that by providing teachers with quality, differentiated training, students will be able to master skills essential to their growth as individuals and to their participation in their communities. As teachers follow training that is practical, scalable, and measurable, they will find increased capacity to meet the growing needs of students, no matter their race, origin, language, or socioeconomic status.
